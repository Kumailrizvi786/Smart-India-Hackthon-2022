Reading as Meditation

We know yoga is the predominant form of meditation. During yoga we are made to be mindful about our body movements and breathing, focusing and controlling every movement with discipline. Which in turn provides an escape from the unwanted thoughts and focus on one self Increasing awareness both self and situational.
We can infer that meditation involves being mindful about some activity like breathing and exercise. We can say that reading engages our mind making it more efficient the way yoga trains our body parts to function to its maximum capacity. While reading through text we are actively involved in constructing characters,defining images,thinking about various scenarios, having expectations, in a way we are constructing a world of our own. Sometimes we are so engrossed in reading that we don't even notice that the stars have replaced the sun in the sky or vice versa.
